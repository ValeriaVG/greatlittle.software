# Privacy Policy

Great Little Software does not store any personal data.
We do not share any data with third parties except for the following subprocessors:

- **PostHog** — for anonymised analytics, see [PostHog Privacy Policy](https://posthog.com/privacy) for details.
- **Buttondown** — for newsletter subscription, see [Buttondown GDPR/EU Compliance](https://buttondown.com/legal/gdpr-eu-compliance) for details.

That is all.
